Release Date: February 2026

============================================
  NEW FEATURE: Global Tax System
============================================

A comprehensive global tax system has been added to DemocracyCore.
This system automatically collects taxes from all active players
on the server and deposits the revenue into the national treasury.

--- Core Mechanics ---

- Automatic tax collection at configurable intervals (default: every 24 hours)
- Configurable tax amount (default: $50 per collection cycle)
- Players offline for 3+ days are automatically exempt from taxation
- All collected taxes are deposited directly into the national treasury
- Full integration with the existing Treasury system

--- Late Payment & Penalty System ---

- Players who cannot afford their taxes accumulate debt
- Outstanding debt incurs a late penalty rate (default: 10% per cycle)
- Partial payments are accepted - remaining balance becomes debt
- Missed payment counter tracks delinquent taxpayers
- Configurable punishment threshold (default: 3 missed payments)
- Customizable punishment commands for tax evasion

--- Punishment System ---

- After exceeding the missed payment threshold, punishments are applied
- Fully configurable punishment commands via config.yml
- Supports placeholders: {player}, {debt}, {missed}
- Players receive on-screen title warnings when punished
- Punishment history tracked per player

--- Custom GUI ---

- New "Global Tax" button added to the main menu (Slot 37, Sunflower icon)
- Dedicated Tax GUI with comprehensive information:
  * Tax System Status (enabled/disabled, total collected, cycles)
  * Tax Rate Information (amount, interval, penalty rate, exemption rules)
  * Treasury Tax Income (revenue breakdown)
  * Next Collection Countdown (time remaining, expected revenue)
  * Collection Statistics (debtors, outstanding debt)
  * Personal Tax Status (player head with full tax history)
  * Debt Status (outstanding debt with warning indicators)
  * Pay Debt Button (one-click debt payment)
  * Tax Transaction History (searchable log)
  * Debtor List (server-wide debtor overview)
- Glowing indicators for players with outstanding debt
- Navigation between Tax GUI and Main Menu

--- Commands ---

Player Commands:
  /dc tax              - Open the Tax GUI
  /dc tax info         - View tax system information
  /dc tax pay          - Pay outstanding tax debt
  /dc tax gui          - Open the Tax GUI

Admin Commands:
  /dc tax admin collect       - Force immediate tax collection
  /dc tax admin enable        - Enable the tax system
  /dc tax admin disable       - Disable the tax system
  /dc tax admin exempt <p>    - Exempt a player from taxes
  /dc tax admin unexempt <p>  - Remove tax exemption
  /dc tax admin forgive <p>   - Forgive a player's tax debt
  /dc tax admin setamount <n> - Set the tax amount

--- Configuration (config.yml) ---

New section added under "global-tax":
  - enabled: true/false
  - amount: Tax amount per cycle (default: 50)
  - collection-interval-hours: Hours between collections (default: 24)
  - inactive-days-exempt: Days offline before exempt (default: 3)
  - late-penalty-rate: Penalty rate on debt (default: 0.10 / 10%)
  - punishment.missed-threshold: Missed payments before punishment (default: 3)
  - punishment.description: Description text for punishment
  - punishment.reset-missed-after-punishment: Reset counter after punishment
  - punishment.commands: List of commands to execute as punishment

--- Permissions ---

New permissions:
  - democracy.tax: View tax information (default: true)
  - democracy.tax.exempt: Exempt from global taxes (default: false)

--- Data Persistence ---

- Tax data saved to data/tax_record.json
- Automatic saving with existing 5-minute autosave cycle
- Per-player tax history, debt tracking, and payment records
- Transaction log (up to 500 entries)

--- Treasury Integration ---

New transaction types added:
  - GLOBAL_TAX_INCOME: Revenue from tax collection
  - TAX_PENALTY_INCOME: Revenue from late penalties

All tax revenue flows directly into the national treasury,
supporting government operations and public spending.

--- Player Notifications ---

- On-join notification if player has outstanding tax debt
- Real-time notification when taxes are collected
- Warning messages for insufficient funds
- Title screen alert for tax evasion punishment
- Server-wide broadcast after each collection cycle
