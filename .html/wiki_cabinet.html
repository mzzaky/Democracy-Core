Overview

Core Module

This document explains in detail the structure, function, and power of the **Cabinet of Ministers** in the server administration. Ministers are the President's right hand who help manage specific aspects of the server.

Key Functionality
There are 5 strategic ministry positions that can be appointed by the President. Each minister has a focus area, special chat prefix, and unique abilities known as Cabinet Decisions.

Cabinet Positions

5 Roles

1. Minister of Defense

Prefix: &4[MoD]

Focus: Server security, PvP, and territory protection.

Responsible for security stability and managing combat events.

2. Minister of Treasury

Prefix: &2[MoT]

Focus: Economy, taxes, and market.

Managing economic stability and distributing wealth.

3. Minister of Commerce

Prefix: &9[MoC]

Focus: Player trade, shops, and resources.

Improving trade activity and availability of rare items.

4. Minister of Infrastructure

Prefix: &7[MoI]

Focus: Building, land claims, and materials.

Facilitating city building and public projects.

5. Minister of Culture

Prefix: &d[MoK]

Focus: Events, entertainment, and community experience.

Keeping community spirit high through festivals and celebrations.

Ministry Powers (Decisions)

Each minister has access to a set of special policies called Cabinet Decisions. These policies have a cost (from the National Treasury) and a specific duration.

Mechanics & Limits

The cabinet system operates under strict rules to ensure fair governance and economic stability.

Appointment & Removal
Only the President has the authority to appoint or dismiss ministers.
Command: /dc cabinet appoint <position> <player>

Other Requirements

  • Cost: Every decision consumes funds directly from the National Treasury.
  • Cooldown: There is a cooldown period between decision usages (Default: 48 Hours per minister).
  • Eligibility: The minister must be currently in office.
  • Funds: The Treasury must have sufficient balance to cover the cost.

Configuration

config.yml

Cost and duration settings can be modified in config.yml under the cabinet.decisions node.

Ensure to restart the server or reload the configuration for changes to take effect.
