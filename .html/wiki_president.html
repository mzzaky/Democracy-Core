Overview

Core Module

The President System module is the heart of DemocracyCore. It simulates a realistic democratic process within the server, handling the entire lifecycle of an election including candidate registration, campaigning, voting, and the final inauguration ceremony.

Key Functionality
The system operates on an automatic cycle determined by the configuration. It ensures fair play through automated checks, anti-cheat voting mechanisms, and transparent data logging.

Election Cycle

4 Phases

The election process is managed by the ElectionManager and is divided into 4 distinct phases.

1. Registration Phase

Duration: 3 Days

Players who meet the strict requirements can register themselves as candidates.

  • Command: /dc register [slogan]
  • Fee: $500,000 (Configurable)

2. Campaign Phase

Duration: 7 Days

Candidates promote themselves to gain support.

  • Endorsements: /dc endorse (+10 Points)
  • Broadcast: /dc campaign ($10k, 6h cooldown)

3. Voting Phase

Duration: 3 Days

Citizens cast their weighted votes for their preferred candidate.

  • Command: /dc vote
  • Rewards: $10k participation + Lottery

4. Inauguration Phase

Duration: 1 Day

Final vote count, winner announcement, and power transfer.

  • Winner: 100% Fee Refund
  • Loser: 50% Fee Refund

Candidate Requirements

To ensure quality leadership, candidates must meet the following strict criteria checked by ElectionManager#meetsRequirements.

Criteria Default Value Description
Minimum Level 100 Player XP/Level must reach this threshold.
Playtime 100 Hours Total recorded playtime on the server.
Balance $500,000 Minimum funds required in Vault account.
Clean Record 30 Days No punishments (bans/mutes) within this period.
Term Limit 2 Terms Cannot serve more than 2 consecutive terms.

Weighted Voting Mechanism

The system utilizes a Weighted Voting mechanism rather than a simple one-person-one-vote system. A player's vote weight depends on their contribution to the server.

The Formula
Total Weight = Base + Playtime Bonus + Level Bonus + Balance Bonus
  • Base Weight: 1.0
  • Playtime Bonus: +0.5 (if playtime > 200 hours)
  • Level Bonus: +0.5 (if level > 75)
  • Balance Bonus: +0.5 (if balance > $1,000,000)
  • Maximum Weight: 2.5

History & Data

Historical data is preserved in PresidentHistory. Every time a term ends—whether by completion, impeachment, or resignation—a new record is archived.

Archived Data Includes:

  • UUID & President Name
  • Term Start & End Dates
  • Approval Rating
  • Number of Executive Orders
  • Event Count
  • Cabinet Members List
  • Accomplishments
  • Termination Reason (Term End, Recall, Inactive, Admin Removal)

Configuration

config.yml

Below are the key configuration nodes available for the President System:

election:
  registration-days: 3
  campaign-days: 7
  voting-days: 3
  
  max-candidates: 3
  registration-fee: 500000
  
  registration-refund-winner: 1.0
  registration-refund-loser: 0.5

  voter-rewards:
    participation: 10000
    lottery-bonus: 50000
    lottery-winners: 10

  vote-weights:
    base: 1.0
    playtime-threshold-hours: 200
    playtime-bonus: 0.5
    level-threshold: 75
    level-bonus: 0.5
    balance-threshold: 1000000
    balance-bonus: 0.5
    max-weight: 2.5
🚨 Emergency Election Triggers
An automatic emergency election will be triggered if:
  • The current president's term has less than 5 days remaining.
  • There is currently no president (Power Vacuum).
  • An admin manually triggers it.
