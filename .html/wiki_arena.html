Overview

PvP Event

The Presidential Arena is a massive Free-For-All (FFA) zone where players can battle each other for cash prizes, rare items, and glory. This event is hosted by the President to distribute wealth and provide entertainment for the citizens.

Event Duration
The event typically lasts for several days (Default: 7 Days), featuring daily leaderboards and a Grand Prize for the ultimate champion.

🔧 Activation

President Only

Only the President has the authority to initiate a new Arena session.

  • Command: /dc arena start
  • Cost: $100,000 (Deducted from National Treasury)
  • Duration: 7 Days (Configurable)
  • Limit: Max 2 times per presidential term
  • Location: Uses the area around World Spawn (Radius: 100 blocks)

🎮 Gameplay & Rules

Combat

Joining & Leaving

  • Join: /dc arena join
    • Saves current location.
    • Clears and saves player inventory.
    • Equips standard Arena Kit.
    • Teleports player to the arena.
  • Leave: /dc arena leave
    • Restores original inventory.
    • Teleports player back to previous location.

Arena Kit (Standard)

All participants receive identical gear to ensure fair play:

  • Full Iron Armor
  • Iron Sword & Bow (32 Arrows)
  • 3x Golden Apple & 16x Cooked Beef

Combat Rules

  • Boundaries: Players leaving the arena radius are teleported back to the center.
  • Respawn: Players respawn at a random location inside the arena after 3 seconds with brief invulnerability.
  • Death Penalty: Dying incurs a $5,000 fine (deducted from personal balance).

💰 Rewards System

Payouts

1. Kill Rewards

Instant reward for each kill:

  • Money: +$5,000 (To Vault Balance)
  • Streak Bonus: Additional rewards for consecutive kills without dying.

2. Killstreak Bonuses

Streak Cash Prize Buffs / Items
5 Kills +$10,000 3x Golden Apple, Speed I (30s)
10 Kills +$25,000 Diamond Sword, Strength I (30s)
25 Kills +$50,000 Netherite Axe, Strength II (60s)
50 Kills +$100,000 Full Netherite Armor, Strength/Resist (2m)

3. Daily Leaderboard

Top 10 players with the most kills each day receive:

  • 1st Place: $100,000 + 3 Netherite Ingots
  • 2nd Place: $75,000 + 2 Netherite Ingots
  • 3rd Place: $60,000 + 2 Netherite Ingots
  • (Rewards decrease down to 10th place)

4. Grand Prize

Awarded to the player with the highest total kills at the end of the event:

  • Cash: $1,000,000
  • Trophy: Arena Champion Trophy (Unique Nether Star)
  • Item: Champion's Chestplate (Special Netherite Chestplate)

⚙️ Configuration

config.yml 
arena:
  # Cost to start the event
  start-cost: 100000
  
  # Event duration (in days)
  duration-days: 7
  
  # Arena radius from spawn (blocks)
  radius: 100
  
  # Base reward per kill
  kill-reward: 5000
  
  # Penalty for dying
  death-penalty: 5000
  
  # Grand prize amount
  grand-prize: 1000000
