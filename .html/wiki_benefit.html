Overview

Rewards System

This document details the personal benefits, buffs, and facilities granted to players holding the position of President or Minister in the DemocracyCore government. These perks are designed to aid officials in their duties and provide tangible rewards for their service.

Key Information
Buffs and salaries are automatically applied and revoked based on the player's current role. They persist across logins but are removed immediately upon leaving office.

🎖️ Presidential Perks

Top Tier

As the supreme leader, the President receives the strongest set of buffs on the server to ensure their security and authority.

1. Status Effects (Permanent)

While in office, the President automatically receives these permanent potion effects:

  • Night Vision: Unrestricted night vision.
  • Saturation: Never get hungry (Hunger bar stays full).

2. Physical Attributes

Enhanced combat and defense capabilities:

Attribute Bonus Description
Attack Damage +15% Increased physical attack damage.
Armor Toughness +1.2 Additional armor resilience (Equivalent to extra Diamond/Netherite).
Max Health +4 Hearts Total of 8 additional HP.

3. Economic & Progress

  • Daily Salary: $5,000 (Automatically deposited daily).
  • Vault Bonus: +20% more money from any source (Mob kills/mining).
  • XP Bonus: +10% Experience gain.

🛡️ Minister Perks

Role Specific

Each minister receives specific buffs tailored to their ministry's role to assist in their daily duties.

Minister of Defense

Role: Combat Commander

  • Strength I: Increased attack damage.
  • Resistance I: Reduced incoming damage.
  • Attributes: +10% Damage, +8% Defense.
  • Salary: $2,500/day.

Minister of Treasury

Role: Money Manager

  • Luck II: Better loot from fishing/chests.
  • Vault Bonus: +15% more money from sources.
  • Salary: $2,500/day.

Minister of Commerce

Role: Fast Trader

  • Speed I: Faster movement for mobilization.
  • Luck I: Moderate luck.
  • Discount: 10% off in server shops (if integrated).
  • Salary: $2,500/day.

Minister of Infrastructure

Role: City Builder

  • Haste II: Instant mining speed (Insta-mine stone/deepslate).
  • Land Claims: +50 Bonus Claim Blocks daily.
  • Salary: $2,500/day.

Minister of Culture

Role: Public Icon

  • Glowing: Glowing effect (Easy visibility at events).
  • Dolphin's Grace: Faster swimming.
  • XP Bonus: +10% Experience gain.
  • Salary: $2,500/day.

⚙️ Buff Mechanisms

System Logic

The system automatically manages the lifecycle of these benefits.

1. Automation

Buffs are automatically applied when a player:

  • Is inaugurated as an official.
  • Logs into the server (Join Event).
  • Respawns after death.

2. Revocation

Buffs are instantly removed if a player:

  • Steps down from office (Term end/Impeachment/Fired).
  • Leaves the server (Quit Event).

🔧 Configuration

config.yml

Buff values and salaries can be adjusted via config.yml:

president:
  buffs:
    damage-bonus: 0.15
    defense-bonus: 0.12
    extra-hearts: 2

treasury:
  salaries:
    president: 5000
    minister: 2500
