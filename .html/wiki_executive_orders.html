Overview

Presidential Power

This document comprehensively explains the Executive Orders system, the highest privilege held by the President to alter global server conditions through official decrees.

Impact
Executive Orders are national-scale policies that have a significant impact on gameplay for all players. Due to their immense power, their usage is limited by high costs from the Treasury and long Cooldown periods.

Usage Rules

Strict protocols govern the issuance of executive orders to maintain balance.

  1. Authority: Only the current President can issue these orders.
  2. Cost: Each order requires a substantial fee (Default: $1,000,000) taken from the Treasury.
    • If treasury funds are insufficient, the order fails.
  3. Cooldown: There is a mandatory waiting period between orders (Default: 7 Days).
  4. Exclusivity: The same order cannot be issued again if it is already active.

List of Executive Orders

Below is the complete list of available executive orders and their technical effects:

Golden Age 48 Hours

A golden era of prosperity.

  • +25% XP Gain (Global)
  • +25% Vault Income (Money)
  • +15% Rare Item Drop Rate
Utility: Boosts general economy and player progress.
State of Emergency 24 Hours

Suspending dangerous activities for safety.

  • PvP Disabled globally (except in official Arenas)
Utility: Prevents chaos or uncontrolled player wars.
National Holiday 24 Hours

A national day off for fun.

  • Jobs Cooldowns removed
  • Daily Rewards can be claimed 2x
Utility: Increases engagement and gives a break from grinding.
War Economy 72 Hours

Economic mobilization for war.

  • PvP Rewards (Kill Rewards) doubled
  • PvP Damage increased by +50%
Utility: Encourages PvP activity and faction conflicts.
Economic Recovery 36 Hours

Massive economic stimulus.

  • $50,000 subsidy to every online player (once)
  • 25% Discount in all server shops
Utility: Helps new players and revives a sluggish economy.
Infrastructure Init. 72 Hours

Accelerating national development.

  • +100 Claim Blocks free
  • Permanent Haste II effect for all players
Utility: Speeds up base or city construction projects.
Environmental Act 48 Hours

Nature protection and fertility.

  • 3x Drop Rate for Farming, Fishing, Breeding
Utility: Benefits farmers and fishermen.
Education Advance 48 Hours

Investment in knowledge.

  • 3x XP Gain
  • Enchanting costs reduced by 50%
  • Free Anvil usage (no XP cost)
Utility: Accelerates leveling and equipment upgrades.
Purge Protocol 6 Hours

⚠️ DANGEROUS! Law of the jungle.

  • PvP active EVERYWHERE (including safezones)
  • Keep Inventory Disabled (items drop on death)
  • Random Respawn locations
Utility: Total chaos event. Extremely high risk.
Presidential Pardon Instant

Mass amnesty.

  • Removes 1 active punishment (warn/mute) from all online players
Utility: Calms the atmosphere or gives second chances.

Technical Mechanism

System

Activation Process

  • Command: /dc order
  • Sequence:
    1. Cost is withdrawn from Treasury.
    2. Cooldown timer starts.
    3. Global message broadcast (+Title screen & Dragon/Wither sound).
    4. Lightning visual effect strikes at President's location.

Global Influence

The ExecutiveOrderManager hooks into various event listeners to modify game parameters in real-time:

  • XP & Drop: Modifies PlayerExpChangeEvent and BlockDropItemEvent.
  • PvP: Cancels or modifies EntityDamageByEntityEvent.
  • Economy: Intervenes in Vault transactions and Shop plugins.

Configuration

Basic parameters can be changed via config.yml:

executive-orders:
  # Cost to issue an order
  cost: 1000000
  
  # Cooldown between orders (days)
  cooldown-days: 7
